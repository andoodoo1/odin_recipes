Wonton Recipe

credits to "Made With Lau"

If you've never had wontons, it's basically a juicy, chewy ball of minced meat folded into a thin sheet of dough.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz wonton wrappers
  • 12 oz pork shoulder
  • 4 oz shrimp
  • 2 pieces green onions

Steps

  1. Chop green onions
  2. Prepare pork
  3. Chope & marinate shrimp
  4. Squeeze & marinate pork
  5. Refrigerate filling
  6. Mix filling
  7. Fold wontons
  8. Boil wontons
