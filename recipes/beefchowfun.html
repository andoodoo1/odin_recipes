Beef Chow Fun Recipe

credits to "Made With Lau"

Chow Fun, also known as Chow Hoh Fun, gets its name from the town of Shahe in Guangzhou, where its distinctive style of flat rice noodles was first created.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz flank steak
  • 1 lb fresh flat rice noodles
  • 3 stems green onions
  • 0.50 yellow onions
  • 1 handful bean sprouts
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Steps

  1. Cut & wash beef
  2. Prepare vegetables
  3. Create beef marinade
  4. Mix beef into marinade
  5. Prepare rice noodles
  6. Create chow fun sauce
  7. Stir-fry beef
  8. Stir-fry vegetables & noodles
  9. Add beef back
  10. Taste test & plate
